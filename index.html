Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a hotel room block?

Yes! We’ve secured a block of rooms at the Mariott. To take advantage of the group discount you must book your room(s) before September 1.

What time should we be there?

The ceremony will begin at 6:30 p.m on October 16. We kindly ask guests to arrive 10-15 minutes prior to grab a glass of bubbly and start mingling.

Is there a dress code?

We hope our wedding will be a fun excuse to wear your favorite cocktail attire!

Any other special attire notes we should know?

Temperatures tend to dip a bit at night, and since the cocktail hour will be outside you might want to bring a jacket or wrap. However there will also be heaters to help keep guests warm.

Will parking be available?

Yes! Valet will be provided free of charge in front of the venue.

Will there be food and drinks served?

Oh yes there will! We will have passed appetizers, a full open bar, as well as a 3-course meal, wedding cake, and desserts! Our main mission is to make you stuffed!.

What if I have a dietary restriction??

Please e-mail us at hello [at] ourwedding.com to let us know! We want you to be safe and comfortable!

How do I contact you if I have other questions?

We’d love to hear from you! Please e-mail us at hello [at] ourwedding.com with any questions, or just to say “hi!” We can’t wait to see you!

Where are you registered at?

Your presence at our wedding is gift enough. Seriously. But because you asked we are registered at Crate & Barrel and Williams Sonoma. Please visit our registry page here (link) for all the details.

Do I have to mail back my RSVP or can I RSVP online?

Please RSVP online.

When should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP by August 31st, 2018

Are there any colours you would prefer me not to wear?

Silver and White

What time is the reception scheduled to end?

12:30am

Are you planning an unplugged ceremony or will guests be allowed to capture the moments on their phones and post to social media? If so, do you have a hashtag for your wedding?

We’ve asked a professional photographer to capture this special moment so that you can sit back, relax and enjoy it with us. Our beautiful images will be available after the wedding.

Can I bring a date?

Oh la la. In order to keep our guest list on track we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. Please feel free to e-mail us with any questions!
